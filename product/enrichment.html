FAQ

What file format do I upload?

Excel (.xlsx). Your file needs at minimum: First Name, Last Name, and either a Date of Birth or Home State. CRD number improves match accuracy significantly for financial advisors.

How long does it take?

Under 5 minutes for 100 contacts. Larger lists of 5,000–14,000 contacts typically process in under 10 minutes. You get the file back automatically when it's done.

What is the C-Score?

Confidence Score. 90 means we found a mobile number via our data partner. 100 means the number already existed in your list and was verified. 0 means no match was found for that contact.

Does this work for financial advisors specifically?

Yes — and it's where we get our best results. Financial advisors are registered with FINRA and have CRD numbers, which gives us a unique anchor to match against personal data records. That's how we hit a 64% mobile match rate on advisor lists where direct dials rarely pick up.
