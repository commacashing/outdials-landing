FAQ

How is this different from other dialers?

Three things: (1) Zero-lag instant bridge - no "dead air" pause when prospects answer. (2) Race Card gives reps a 5-second heads-up so they're prepared before the call connects. (3) We go live in 7 days, not 30. No heavy CRM packages, just clean OAuth2.

Is this TCPA compliant?

100%. Hard-coded consent checks, time-zone enforcement (8 AM - 9 PM local), automatic DNC filtering, and full audit trails. Every call requires human initiation, and reps are instantly bridged - zero abandoned calls. Built specifically for regulated industries like insurance and financial services.

How long does implementation take?

7 days from contract to live dials. Day 1-2: CRM OAuth setup and field mapping. Day 3-4: Testing and AMD calibration. Day 5: Pilot with 2-3 reps. Day 6-7: Full team rollout. Most competitors take 14-30 days.

What if we already have a dialer?

OutDials plugs into your existing CRM instance. Your data stays exactly where it is, your workflows don't change - you just get faster dialing. If you're on a contract with another provider, we can run a 7-day pilot alongside it to prove the lift before you switch.
